Our Technology

Our system utilizes cutting-edge computer vision and machine learning to provide real-time sign language interpretation. By capturing hand movements with a camera, our technology translates sign language gestures into easily understandable text, fostering communication and accessibility.

Harnessing the power of MediaPipe and Python, our sign language recognition system offers advanced translation capabilities. The system accurately detects hand landmarks, processes spatial data, and employs a trained model to predict sign language characters, delivering a robust and efficient interpretation solution.

Why Choose Us?

Globally, over 1.5 billion people, or nearly 20% of the world's population, live with hearing loss. Of these, 430 million have disabling hearing loss. By 2050, this number is projected to rise to over 700 million, impacting 1 in 10 people.

We are dedicated to bridging communication gaps through our innovative sign language interpretation technology. Our system empowers users to connect more effectively by converting sign language into text in real-time, promoting inclusivity and facilitating seamless interaction.

Cost-Effective Solution

Our sign language interpretation system offers a cost-effective alternative to traditional in-person interpreting services. By leveraging technology to automate the translation process, we can significantly reduce the expenses associated with scheduling, travel, and hourly fees for human interpreters, making communication more accessible to a wider range of individuals and organizations.

Our technology provides a scalable and cost-effective solution for sign language interpretation. Unlike traditional methods, which require a one-to-one interpreter-to-user ratio, our system can serve multiple users simultaneously. This scalability drives down the cost per user, making effective communication more attainable for institutions and individuals with budget constraints.
